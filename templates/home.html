    • Our Mission

    Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare record management by providing a secure, efficient, and accessible
    digital filing system for public clinics within the health department. Through innovative technology
    and a commitment to excellence, we aim to streamline record-keeping processes, enhance data security and
    privacy, and improve the overall quality of healthcare services. By empowering healthcare professionals with timely
    access to patient information and fostering a patient-centered approach, we strive to positively impact patient outcomes and experiences
    while advancing the delivery of healthcare in our community.

    About

    As a former filing clerk intern under the Department of Health, I observed firsthand the challenges patients face when queuing
    for long hours to retrieve their medical records.
    This issue is particularly detrimental to patients requiring regular follow-up visits, such as those managing HIV and TB conditions.
    Prolonged waiting times not only inconvenience patients but also discourage them from seeking necessary
    medical care, including collecting their medication. By transitioning to a
    digital filing system, we aim to alleviate the burden on patients and improve their
    overall experience with healthcare services. Quicker access to medical records means shorter waiting times,
    reducing patient frustration and increasing the likelihood of follow-up visits.
